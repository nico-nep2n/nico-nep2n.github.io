About

Nico nep2n

nico nep2n is an emerging alternative synth-pop artist creating ethereal, spacey soundscapes that feel like stepping into another dimension. As he blends genres to create a sound uniquely his own, his music explores themes of love, introspection, and justice, bringing listeners deeper into his world. With releases like “space” and “don’t turn me off,” he pushes to bring a new definition of what an independent artist can be.
